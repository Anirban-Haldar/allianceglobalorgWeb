About Alliance Global

Alliance Global is a premier export firm from India, founded by Capt. Ashim Kumar Chakraborty & Ananya Chakraborty.

Founded with a vision to deliver excellence, Alliance Global is your one-stop solution for high-quality herbal extracts, spices, and textiles, along with other carefully curated products.

Our Purpose & Direction

Mission & Vision

Mission
Our mission is to deliver high-quality products at competitive prices while bringing products from the remotest regions of India to the global market. We aim to highlight the craftsmanship and dedication of local producers while fostering an ecosystem supported by sustainable and environmentally responsible technologies.
We are committed to continuous improvement, operational growth, and employee development, while maintaining profitability for the benefit of our customers, employees, and community.
Our goal is to create a global ecosystem where authentic Indian products are easily accessible, uplifting the livelihoods of hardworking communities across the country.
Vision
Our vision is to achieve the highest level of customer satisfaction among exporters in India, driven by a strong commitment to quality and the strength of our people.
We aspire to see Indian products reach every corner of the world while promoting fair trade and sustainable practices through collaborative planning and shared responsibility within our team.
We are dedicated to ethical sourcing, ensuring that every product we export meets the highest standards of safety, integrity, and trust.
